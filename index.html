About Us

At Darajaat Integrated Schools, we are more than educators; we are cultivators of dreams and builders of character. Our passion lies in creating a nurturing environment where children don't just learn—they thrive, explore, and grow into well-rounded individuals. Guided by ethical values and a commitment to excellence, we empower students to excel academically, emotionally, and spiritually.

Our Vision

We envision a generation of lifelong learners who lead with integrity, wisdom, and compassion. Our mission is to inspire positive change in the global community by fostering academic excellence, ethical leadership, and a love for learning.

Discover the Perfect Fit for Your Child

Step 1: Explore Our Programs

Students of all faiths can enroll in our Cambridge International Curriculum, which provides a globally recognized academic foundation. For Muslim students, we offer additional tailored options: Integrated Curriculum (for students enrolled in our day school, combining Cambridge academics with optional Islamic studies), Weekend and Holiday Madrassa (exclusively for Muslim students not enrolled in our day school, focusing on Islamic studies outside regular hours), and the Tahfeedh Program (open to all Muslim students regardless of enrollment status, for Quran memorization). Parents can select programs individually or combine them to suit their child’s needs.

Step 2: Let’s Meet Your Star!

For students entering KS2 and above, we invite your child for a friendly and engaging interview. This is a wonderful opportunity for us to get to know your child and assess their readiness for the next step in their educational journey.
Note: Foundation classes do not require an interview – we’re ready to welcome your little one with open arms!

Step 3: Secure Your Child’s Spot!

Once you’ve chosen your program and completed the interview (if applicable), it’s time to finalize the admission process. Simply pay the admission fee and submit the completed admission form to secure your child’s place at Darajaat..
